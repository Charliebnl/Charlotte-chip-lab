# CHIP — Best Results

This page showcases examples of CHIP outputs.

## Example 1 — 4-Week Coaching Plan

**Input:**
- Level 2.5 player
- Right side
- Struggles with volleys and positioning
- Plays 3 times per week

**Output (summary):**

### 🎯 Player Snapshot
- Intermediate level
- Reactive positioning
- Inconsistent net presence

### ⚠️ Key Issues
- Late preparation on volleys
- Poor recovery after shots
- Over-committing to low-percentage balls

### 🔧 Priority Fixes
1. Recover immediately after every volley
2. Hold position and do not over-commit
3. Slow down decisions under pressure

### 🎯 Next Match Focus
- After every volley, reset with one recovery step

## Example 2 — Short Clip Guidance

**Input:**
- Short clip under 2 minutes
- Player provides context on level, side, and struggle

**Output (summary):**
- Coaching guidance based on visible patterns
- Practical next steps
- Focus on positioning, recovery, and decision-making
- No claim of full technical or biomechanical analysis

---

## What this demonstrates

CHIP:
- prioritises high-impact improvements
- structures development clearly
- gives practical, scannable coaching
- works best with player-provided context
